\begin{cvletter}

\lettersection{Why Apply to the MDE?}
I am excited to apply for this research consultant positon and work directly with MDE. My career aspiration is to advance the state of educational measurement and research for the benefit of all students, especially those currently underserved. I believe that this position in the Office of Systems, Evaluation, and Technology serves that end.

In a previous position as a graduate research assistant I collaborated with staff in the MiSTEM Network to evaluate their programs and assess their progress towards yearly goals. I am passionate about the role of measurement and evidence in improving education outcomes for all students.

\lettersection{Why Me?}
In my graduate program, I developed my interest in the role of evidence in educational policy and practice. I learned how to conduct systematic reviews and meta-analyses of published studies to provide the best possible recommendations. I have created research protocols to characterize study designs and evaluate their rigor. To improve the transparency and credibility of my research, I self-taught how to program in R and the basics of data management. 

For me, learning new skills and developing a diverse skill set is an especially enjoyable aspect of research. During my time as a research assistant evaluating educational programs, I engaged in both qualitative (interviews and case studies) and quantitative (descriptive and inferential statistics) methods to draft annual reports of NSF and MDE-funded programs, collaborating with internal and external team members.

\lettersection{What Can I Contribute?}
Aside from my interests, skills, and experience referred to above and in my CV, I bring a developmental and collaborative approach to my work. I have cultivated relationships and collaborations in time as a graduate student, even organizing a new research group for fellow students. I believe that good work is transparent, accessible, and therefore inclusive of current and future colleagues.

I believe that my focus on collaboration, extensive research background, and two years of experience in education evaluation would make me a valuable part of the team. I am grateful for the opportunity to apply and be considered for this position.
\end{cvletter}
