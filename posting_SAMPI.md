## General Summary	
Science and Mathematics Program Improvement (SAMPI) at Western Michigan University (WMU) is seeking a Post-Graduate Fellow who is *passionate about improving STEM education research, teaching, and learning.* This individual will be primarily working on our grant contract project with the MiSTEM Network, an organization funded by the State of Michigan. The MiSTEM Network supports implementation of project, problem, and place-based education in K-12 and partnerships between K-12 schools and businesses, non-profits, and community organizations. Responsibilities include *database management, literature reviews, developing and implementing quantitative/qualitative data collection protocols, data analysis, report writing, and communicating with external clients*. We are seeking applicants who *have experience and/or interested evaluation, research, and project management in STEM education.*
